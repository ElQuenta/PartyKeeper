Templar Gladiator Enemy Class Eldritch / Beast Size Large (2) Actions per round Stats HP 137 HP (Stygian/Bloodmoon) 165 Dodge 46.25% Protection 0% Speed 5 Resistances Stun 97.5% Blight 97.5% Bleed 67.5% Debuff 67.5% Move 73% The Templar Gladiator is a weaker version of the Templar Impaler which can be encountered on the third quest in the Darkest Dungeon . Unlike the Impaler, it has only one action per turn, and lacks the hard-hitting Revelation ability, which makes it much easier to deal with - but its remaining abilities can still do a lot of damage if you get careless. Use stuns and debuffs to keep it in check and prevent it from attacking.
