Bone Captain Enemy Type Unholy Size Large Actions per round Stats Variation Bone Commander Bone General HP 49 68 HP (Stygian/Bloodmoon) 59 82 Dodge 8.75% 21.25% Protection 33% 33% Speed 1 2 Stealth No No Resistances Stun 70% 90% Blight 30% 50% Bleed 220% 240% Debuff 40% 60% Move 95% 115% The Bone Captain is an enemy that appears in Veteran-tier or harder expeditions in the Ruins . These undead soldiers, befitting their rank, are tough and strong, using their large maces to clobber the heroes. The weight of their strikes can cause the very ground to quake, leaving those affected dazed and reeling. Contents 1 Behavior 2 Abilities 3 Related Enemies 3.1 All Areas 3.2 Ruins 3.3 Boss 4 Gallery Behavior The Captain is a Large Unholy monster that always appears in front line of its party with ranged/support undead and/or cultists behind him. Its main ability is Ground Pound, a mighty smash that can stun your entire party. Sometimes it can perform a single, bone-crushing strike aimed at one of your front-line troopers, dealing massive damage and making it more likely that they will be stunned. Abilities This enemy does not appear in Apprentice level dungeons.
